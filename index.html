02 FOCUS
 
 
 
 

URBAN ACTIVITIES

The community from Vparken has founded the Urban Academy for children and young people who are interested in music, performance and dance. The place forms the framework for a sound studio, a dance hall and venue.

Urban Dance

Urban Dance is a professional teaching in various urban dance styles. Urban Dance creates a framework for young people in Vejleå Park to become part of a creative community with great development opportunities.

Urban Music

Urban Music is professional music education in the Hip Hop genre. Here, young people have the opportunity to become part of an ambitious music universe.

Urban Explore

Urban Explore strengthens the creative skills of children and young people by creating a wide range of positive leisure facilities initiated based on the children's own demand and in collaboration with local associations.

Urban Voice

Urban Voice is a child and youth council who creates a developing network. Urban Voice works to strengthen active citizenship, democracy and community among children and young people in Vejleå Parken.

Neighborhood Watch

The purpose of neighboring aid is to reduce burglary and maintain the security of housing, working together with Criminal Prevention Council and Ishøj Municipality.

Fire Cadet

The fire brigade provides twice a year, 8 young residents in Vejleå Park with a chance to participate in a 5 day event at a fire station. Here, the young people learn useful tools for real life situations.

Financial Advice

Several days a week, a municipal employee sits and advises the residents of the residential area on a wide range of doubts and questions regarding the birocracy which the residents might meet.

Prolonged Guidance

Lisbeth Hellmers is an UU supervisor who guides young people in their education and up to 25 years and their parents about education.

Business and Youth Studies

Young people and their parents, get knowledge of what it takes to get a youth education and what courses are realistic with their children's professional level.

Allowance Project

Pocket money projects are a key tool in educational support and a pocket job can help the youth to find a future education or job for themselves.

Family Academy

The family academy is run for vulnerable families with children between 0-6 years. The purpose is that more parents feel better equipped for parenting and thus better give their children better opportunities to thrive.

Parents Network

The parents' network is an offer for parents in need of support and sparring in family life's various challenges. An important part of the work is to strengthen the parent's skills and equip them to take care of the family in vulnerable situation.
